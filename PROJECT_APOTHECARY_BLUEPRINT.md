# ⚗️ PROJECT APOTHECARY: THE FLUID ENGINE

**Objective**: Manufacture high-purity Ethanol and Bio-Diesel from waste.
**Triple Threat**: Antiseptic (Medical) | Fuel (Mechanical) | Currency (Trade).

---

## 🏗️ 1. THE SCAVENGER'S STILL (Ethanol)

*Convert sugar/fruit into high-proof fuel.*

### Equipment (Scavenged)

- **The Boiler**: Pressure cooker or heavy pot with sealed lid.
- **The Condenser**: Copper cooling coils from a refrigerator back.
- **The Seal**: Flour + Water paste (Rye dough). Hardens like concrete, non-toxic.

### The Protocol

1. **Ferment**: Mash sugar/fruit in a bucket. Add yeast. Wait 7 days (Bubbling stops).
2. **Boil**: Heat mash in Boiler. Ethanol boils at **78°C (172°F)**. Water boils at 100°C.
3. **Condense**: Vapor hits the copper coil (submerged in cold water) and drips out as liquid.
4. **The Cut**: Discard first 50ml (**Methanol/Poison**). Keep the rest (**Ethanol/Heart**).

## 🩺 2. THE MEDICAL APOTHECARY

*Turn the Still into a Pharmacy.*

- **Sterilization**: 70% Ethanol is the standard for cleaning Dental Forceps (150/151).
- **Pain Relief (Willow Tincture)**:
  - *Source*: Inner white bark of Willow tree.
  - *Process*: Soak in ethanol for 6 weeks.
  - *Result*: Liquid Aspirin (Salicin).
- **Wound Care (Pine Resin)**:
  - *Source*: Warm Pine Resin + Animal Fat/Beeswax.
  - *Result*: Antibiotic "Liquid Bandage".

## ⛽ 3. THE FUEL SYSTEM

*Powering the Monolith when gas stations die.*

- **Flex-Fuel**: Most modern engines run on **E85** (85% Acetone/Ethanol mix).
- **Penny Stove**: Two aluminum cans + ethanol = 5-minute water boil.
- **Bio-Diesel (The EarthRoamer Fuel)**:
  - *Ingredients*: Waste Veggie Oil (Restaurants) + Methanol (Heet) + Lye (Wood Ash).
  - *Process*: Filter oil -> Mix with Lye/Methanol -> Settle -> Drain Glycerin -> **Fuel**.

**COMMANDER**: Providing the ability to heal, burn, and trade.
