* **Simulation:** Every `Potential_Vector` runs through the Legal Simulator:

  * *Is it compliant?* (Stripe/PayPal prohibited items check).
* **Result:** "Counterfeit Sneakers" -> **KILL**. "White-label Generic" -> **APPROVE**.
