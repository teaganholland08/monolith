## 1. 🇨🇦 GOVERNMENT "FREE MONEY" (Immediate Claims)

### **A. BC Income Assistance (The Floor)**

* **Value:** Up to **$935 - $1,358 per month** (Single Employable).
* **Action:**
    1. Go to: [My Self Serve](https://myselfserve.gov.bc.ca/)
    2. Register using SIN.
    3. **Upload:** Your Birth Certificate.
    4. **Copy-Paste:** The text from `BC_ASSISTANCE_STATEMENT.md` into the "Need" section.
    5. **Status:** Applications usually processed in 1-2 weeks.

### **B. Canada Workers Benefit (CWB)**

* **Value:** Up to **$1,515 / year**.
* **Action:** This is claimed automatically when we file your taxes. The system tracks this via `accountant_agent.py`.
* **Advance Payments:** You can request up to 50% in advance payments via CRA My Account.

### **C. GST/HST Credit**

* **Value:** **$120 - $150** quarterly.
* **Next Payout:** October 5th, January 5th.
* **Action:** Automatic upon tax filing.
